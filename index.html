We are operatic soloists, choral scholars, organists, pianists and harpists and can offer a wide range of options from a simple soloist to duo, trio and quartet and larger ensembles.  We are happy to perform with your pre-arranged organist or bring our own professional organist, pianist, harpist or even perform a cappella for you.  We will work with you to ensure that you are happy with the songs and format of music for your very special day. With a huge repertoire of traditional and non-religious song as well as performance formats, we can create the perfect musical backdrop to your church or civil service. If requested we can also provide suggestions based on any details you have about the perfect music to suit your taste and style.

Where musical accompaniment is concerned, our wedding singers are very experienced at performing with pianists, organists, choir, a cappella, string quartet, and more. We are happy to work with musicians whom you’ve arranged independently, or to organise accompaniment per your request. If you are unsure of what you’d like to have performed, we are happy to talk you through the options based on what you love.
